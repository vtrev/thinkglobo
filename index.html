How we can help you



Training design

We design, develop and implement training that sticks. Our research-based programs are interactive, collaborative and creative.

Participants will easily apply their practical knowledge and skills immediately. We have expertise in face-to-face designs, e-learning and blended programs.



Facilitation

We are at the cutting edge with the latest knowledge and skills in adult learning, facilitation methods, needs assessment, and, measuring the impact of learning programs.

Our approach is holistic and we cater to your individual experiences. Our solutions are not generic, we enhance performance through improved emotional and social skills. We believe that what you learn is as important as how you learn.



Program co-design

We are solution focused. Using a human-centred design approach we co-design effective programmatic solutions that address strategic planning, leadership development and gender mainstreaming.




Why you will love working with us: At thinkglobo we meet our clients where they are- we listen to what they really need and want.

Innovative

We like to do things differently. By creating deep relationships with our clients we help them design out-of-the box solutions to real-world problems.

Creative

We like to play. We create an environment for ideas to flow freely without judgement. Deliberate creative problem solving will help you stay competitive in today's ever-changing world.

Impactful

We like to make a difference. Only when our solutions are sustainable and truly improve the lives and work of others are we satisfied.
